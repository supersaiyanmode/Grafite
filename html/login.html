Grafite

What's this?

Grafite is a simple service that allows you to get the latest activity on all of your social networks, at one place that you like the most -- chat window! As of now, the following networks are supported

  • Facebook
  • Google Buzz
  • Twitter

To get started, register yourself. To register click here

Catch Grafite Bot at Google Talk ID grafiteapp@appspot.com.

More detailed instructions here.
